About Us

Cricket Star Academy

Cricket Star Academy provides the foremost nurturing grounds for young talent (Under 10, 13, & 16).The goal was to become the trigger for new talent in the country, particularly for praiseworthy boys that don’t have a platform.The objective of this cricket academy in Karachi is to provide the best-quality cricket training, a world-class cricket infrastructure, and the chance to train cricket experts in a fun and creative atmosphere under the guidance of qualified and experienced coaches. Our objective to create a sense in students for Working Hard, Commitment, Honesty, Teamwork, Equality, Accountability, Respect, Reciprocity, Transparency,and Excellence.

Vision

The vision behind the Cricket Star Academy is that of a journey of continuous improvement and advancement. To support the country’s young talent to master and keep ahead of the game.

Mission

The Mission of Cricket Star Academy is to provide professional training for young competitors to fulfill their maximum capabilities and to teach sports leadership skills and motivate them to turn into future players.
